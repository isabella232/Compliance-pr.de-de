## <a name="how-does-microsoft-365-manage-the-keys-used-for-encryption"></a><span data-ttu-id="1b05f-122">Wie verwaltet Microsoft 365 die für die Verschlüsselung verwendeten Schlüssel?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1b05f-122">How does Microsoft 365 manage the keys used for encryption?</span></span>
